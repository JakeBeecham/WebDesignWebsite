Background

Overwatch. An international task force that maintained peace for a generation before it was shut down. Now in the wake of its dismantling, global conflict is rising. Overwatch may be gone… but the world still needs heroes. Overwatch is a colorful team-based shooter video game starring a diverse cast of powerful heroes. Travel the world, build a team, and contest objectives in exhilarating 6v6 combat.

Award Winning Franchise

Overwatch released on the 24th of May 2016, preceeding a very successful Public Beta Test, in which players were able to get their hands on the game early, for testing. Since then, the developers of Overwatch, Blizzard Entertainment, have got Overwatch nominated for and have won, various awards over the years for its new and innovative 6v6 first person shooter game. The lists below show the various awards Overwatch has been nominated for and also the awards Overwatch has won since its release.

2016

  • Winner - The Game Awards, Game Award, Game of the Year
  • Winner - HMMA Awards, HMMA Award, Best Original Score - Video Game
  • Nominee - HMMA Awards, HMMA Award, Best Song/Score - Trailer

2017

  • Winner - NAVGTR Awards, NAVGTR Award, Character Design & Game of the Year & Game - eSports & Game - Original Action
  • Nominee - NAVGTR Awards, NAVGTR Award, Game Design - New IP
  • Winner - BAFTA Awards, Games Award, Best Multiplayer

2018

  • Nominee - The Game Awards, Game Award, Best Ongoing Game
  • Winner - NAVGTR Awards, NAVGTR Award, Game - eSports
  • Winner - BAFTA Awards, Games Award, Best Evolving Game

2019

  • Nominee - The Game Awards, Game Award, Esports Game of the Year
  • Nominee - NAVGTR Awards, NAVGTR Award, Game - eSports
  • Nominee - BAFTA Awards, Game Award, Best Evolving Game

Overwatch V Overwatch 2

Overwatch is a Multiplayer 6v6 Team-Based Shooter. Overwatch features an international cast of 32 powerful heroes with captivating personalities and backstories. Along with this, Overwatch includes 21 core maps from diverse locations around the world, from the technological marvels of Busan to the snowy slopes of Nepal, every map has objectives, secrets, and strategies to explore. As this is the multiplayer side of the game, players who do not want to play the new singleplayer story mode, do not have to upgrade to Overwatch 2, as Overwatch will link to Overwatch 2 in being Overwatch 2's multiplayer mode. Overwatch players will be able to see and play along side the new updated visuals to heros, but will not be able to have the updated visuals as this requires upgrading to Overwatch 2.

Overwatch 2 builds on an award-winning foundation and challenges the world’s heroes to team up, power up, and take on an overwhelming outbreak of threats around the globe. Overwatch 2 is a hybrid of Overwatch. It includes everything that Overwatch has, along with adding a brand new singleplayer story mode. It will include various new heroes and maps at launch, adding to the roster of heroes and maps of Overwatch. It will also include new updated visuals to all current and future heroes of the game. Current Overwatch players will battle side-by-side with Overwatch 2 players in PvP multiplayer, along with being able to play Overwatch 2 heroes and maps. Accomplishments and loot collections of Overwatch players will be carried forward to Overwatch 2. That means Overwatch players will keep their skins, player icons, sprays, emotes, and more!
