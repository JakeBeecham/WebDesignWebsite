Overwatch

Background

Overwatch. An international task force that maintained peace for a generation before it was shut down. Now in the wake of its dismantling, global conflict is rising. Overwatch may be gone… but the world still needs heroes. Overwatch is a colorful team-based shooter game starring a diverse cast of powerful heroes. Travel the world, build a team, and contest objectives in exhilarating 6v6 combat.

Overwatch V Overwatch 2

Overwatch is a Multiplayer 6v6 Team-Based Shooter. Overwatch features an international cast of 31 powerful heroes with captivating personalities and backstories. Along with this, Overwatch includes 28 maps from diverse locations around the world, from the technological marvels of Busan to the snowy slopes of Nepal, every map has objectives, secrets, and strategies to explore. As this is the multiplayer side of the game, players who do not want to play the new singleplayer story mode, do not have to upgrade to Overwatch 2, as Overwatch will link to Overwatch 2 in being Overwatch 2's multiplayer mode. Overwatch players will be able to see and play along side the new updated visuals to heros, but will not be able to have the updated visuals as this requires upgrading to Overwatch 2.

Overwatch 2 builds on an award-winning foundation and challenges the world’s heroes to team up, power up, and take on an overwhelming outbreak of threats around the globe. Overwatch 2 is a hybrid of Overwatch. It includes everything that Overwatch has, along with adding a brand new singleplayer story mode. It will include various new heros and maps at launch, adding to the roster of heros and maps of Overwatch. It will also include new updated visuals to all current and future heros of the game. Current Overwatch players will battle side-by-side with Overwatch 2 players in PvP multiplayer, along with being able to play Overwatch 2 heroes and maps. Accomplishments and loot collections of current Overwatch players will be carried forward to Overwatch 2. That means current Overwatch players will keep their skins, player icons, sprays, emotes, and more!
