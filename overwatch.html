Heroes of Overwatch

THE WORLD NEEDS HEROES! Overwatch features an international cast of powerful heroes with captivating personalities and backstories. When you enter a game of Overwatch, you'll need to choose a hero to play. Every hero is unique and each have a unique set of devastating abilities, from a cannon-toting ape from the moon, a time-traveling freedom fighter, a beat-dropping battlefield DJ, a security guard that rains destruction from above in a jet-powered armor suit or a genetically enhanced hamster piloting a superpowered hamster ball. Whoever you want to be, there's an Overwatch hero for you. In Overwatch, there are 3 roles that all the heroes fall into, they include, Tank, Damage and Support. Find out more about the Heroes of Overwatch here...

Hero Roles

Tank

There are 8 Tank Heroes currently in-game. Tank heroes soak damage and shatter fortified positions. They like closely grouped enemies and narrow chokepoints. If you’re a tank, you lead the charge.

Damage

There are 17 Damage Heroes currently in-game. Damage heroes seek out, engage, and obliterate the enemy with wide-ranging tools, abilities and play styles. Fearsome but fragile, these heroes require backup to survive.

Support

There are 7 Support Heroes currently in-game. Support heroes empower their allies by healing, shielding, boosting damage, and disabling foes. As a support, you’re the backbone of your team’s survival.

Maps of Overwatch

In Overwatch, heroes do battle in diverse locations around the world. From the technological marvels of Busan to the snowy slopes of Nepal, every map has objectives, secrets, and strategies to explore. There are 4 core game modes or map types in Overwatch. These are, Control, Assault, Escort and Hybrid. Each map in Overwatch is made to align with 1 of the 4 map types. There are currently 21 unique maps in-game, set in diverse locations around the world. Find out more about the Maps of Overwatch here...

Map Types

Control

Teams fight to hold a single objective in the middle of the map. The first team to win two rounds wins the map.

Assault

Attackers fight to capture a series of objectives around the map. Defenders hold them off until time runs out.

Escort

Attackers escort a payload around the map to a delivery point, while defenders strive to stop them.

Hybrid

Hybrid is a combination of map types Assault and Escort. Attackers capture a payload, then escort it to its destination. Defenders try to hold them back.
